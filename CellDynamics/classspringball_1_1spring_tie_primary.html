Detailed Description

A spring tying some ball to some ball in (generally) another set.

Force applies to either one (if both ball_num values are the same) or two balls in this set, and a single ball in the other set. This force can be unattached, in which case it is a no-op.

Caveat:
This is paired with a springTieMimic to ensure the forces are equal and opposite. The Mimic must be applied after this has populated it.
